The “ambition loop” is a positive feedback loop in which bold government policies and private sector leadership reinforce each other, and together take sustainable action to the next level.

About us

Our organization is dedicated to advancing the SDGs global agenda working on topics like (and not limited to) climate change, encompassing mitigation, adaptation, resilience, and climate finance, as well as biodiversity conservation and the mitigation of plastics pollution. Central to our vision is the steadfast commitment to fostering multilateral collaboration and aiding multilateral organizations, civil society, national, and sub-national states in addressing these pressing global challenges, as well as the firm belief that the delivery of solutions to global challenges can only be achieved with the mobilization of the private sector at scale.

"We recognize that the roots of the concepts is based in the Paris Agreement and was properly picked by We Mean Business and the High Level Champions, as well as other UN negotiation that are willing to create a positive interaction with all non party stakeholders."

Our Mission

Ambition Loop is driven by a profound sense of responsibility towards the planet and its inhabitants. Our mission is to lead and inspire coordinated, strategic action on (but not limited to) climate change, biodiversity conservation, plastics pollution, sustainable food systems, water, and other related to environmental and social sustainability, at the local, national, and global levels. We are dedicated to promoting a sustainable and resilient future for all.

Commitments

Through these commitments, we strive to fulfill our founder's vision of creating a world where the global community works together harmoniously to combat climate change, protect biodiversity, and eliminate plastics pollution and address inequity. We are resolute in our dedication to these causes and vow to pursue them with unwavering determination, integrity, and the spirit of collaboration.

Climate Change

Recognizing the urgent need for climate action, we commit to:

  • Advocating for policies that reduce greenhouse gas emissions and advance clean energy solutions.
  • Facilitating climate adaptation and resilience initiatives, particularly in vulnerable communities.
  • Fostering innovative climate finance mechanisms to promote sustainable development and climate resilience.

Biodiversity Conservation

Biodiversity is the foundation of thriving ecosystems. Our organization pledges to:

  • Raise awareness about the critical importance of biodiversity conservation.
  • Collaborate with experts and organizations to protect endangered species and habitats.
  • Advocate for policies that harmonize development with the preservation of biodiversity.

Plastic's Pollution

Plastics pollution poses a grave threat to our planet. We commit to:

  • Raising awareness about the environmental consequences of plastics pollution
  • Supporting initiatives to reduce harming single-use plastics and promote recycling.
  • Collaborating with stakeholders to innovate solutions for a plastics-pollution-free future.

Global economic equity

None of the above areas will achieve breakthrough transformation without the deployment of significant multiples of current financial flows. This requires the evolution of all aspects of the global economy and financial system: bigger, more agile and higher leverage development finance; more mature, bolder and more supportive domestic resource mobilisation; and a far greater role for international private finance in emerging and developing economies.
All the rules of the game will need to change to make this happen.

Radical Collaboration

We believe that solutions to global challenges demand global and radical cooperation. We pledge to:

  • Foster multilateral collaboration among governments, civil society, and multilateral organizations.
  • Provide expertise and support to multilateral organizations engaged in environmental work.
  • Advocate for inclusive, cooperative approaches that transcend borders and encompass national and sub-national states.
  • Activate the positive ambition loop between governmental and business leadership wherever possible - no systemic change is possible without both these forces being activated
